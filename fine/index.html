About Us

Peculiar Restaurant is your number one stop for mouth-watering Ugandan and international dishes. Founded in 2015, we have served thousands of happy customers with meals made from love, tradition, and excellence.

Our Mission: To serve delicious, affordable food that guests will want to return to week after week.

Our Vision: To be the leading restaurant brand known for quality service and cultural hospitality.
